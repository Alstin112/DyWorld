Chalcopyrite: Copper, Zinc, Sulfur
Hematite: Iron, Tin, Nickel
Galena: Lead, small amount Silver, small amount Gold
Prolycotherium: Chromium, Lithium, Tungsten
Carbolycite: Coal, Carbon, Sulfur
Bauxite: Stone, Aluminium, small amount Gold
Radicium: Uranium

Beginning of the game you need your 4 basic resources: Stone (Bauxite), Coal (Carbolycite), Copper (Chalcopyrite) and Iron (Hematite). These can be used directly as their normal ores. So for example, Bauxite is stone in recipes. Simple as that.
